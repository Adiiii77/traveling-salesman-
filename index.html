Professor

Mr. Jaydeep Kotadiya

Jaydeep Kotadiya is an accomplished Ceramic Art teacher at Surat College of Fine Art, VNSGU, with a Master of Fine Arts in Ceramic & Glass from Vishwa Bharti University. Since 2019, he has served as a Teaching Assistant, blending his academic expertise with hands-on artistry. As the owner of Mudberry Pottery Studio, he extends his passion for ceramic art to the community, fostering creativity and learning.
